# NIST AI RMF — Compliance Checklist

**Framework:** NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)  
**Published by:** U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (2023)  
**Applicable to:** Organizations developing, deploying, or procuring AI systems

---

## How to Use This Checklist
- ☐ = Not started
- 🔄 = In progress  
- ✅ = Completed
- N/A = Not applicable to your organization

---

## GOVERN — Organizational Practices

| # | Control | Reference | Status | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| G1 | AI risk management policy is documented and approved | GOVERN 1.1 | ☐ | |
| G2 | Roles and responsibilities for AI oversight are defined | GOVERN 1.2 | ☐ | |
| G3 | AI governance committee or owner is assigned | GOVERN 1.3 | ☐ | |
| G4 | Staff are trained on AI risk awareness | GOVERN 1.4 | ☐ | |
| G5 | AI procurement process includes risk evaluation | GOVERN 1.5 | ☐ | |
| G6 | Organizational AI values and principles are documented | GOVERN 1.6 | ☐ | |
